JAIREL

A game of hordes with Brazilian Folklore theme featuring characters like: Mula sem cabeça, Boi Tatá, Boto cor de Rosa, Chico Bento (player). The initial idea is to bring a game in the style of "space invaders" mixed with hordes per level in the "swamp attack" style, plus the "tetris" mechanic where defeat is sealed when enemies cross a boundary line.

Backstory

The ultra-capitalist John Biggle (who strangely dresses as a pirate or cowboy or whatever else you can imagine) is using symbols of Brazilian folklore for his production purposes. Stop him with the help of Chico Bento stunning and freeing the animals from John's clutches with his incredible Guava bazooka.


Link for the Github repository: https://github.com/alvim-coltec/jairel
